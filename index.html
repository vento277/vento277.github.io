About Build Write
Hey, thanks for dropping by.

I am a senior electrical engineering student at the University of British Columbia. My current focus is on building products and conducting research geared towards fostering sustainability. Some of my recent projects include research on Energy Usage Intensity Forecasting and developing firmware for an Autonomous Research Sailboat.

Outside of engineering, I read about investments, drawing insights from Buffett’s partnership letters, Howard Marks’s memos, and Charlie’s occasional gems to broaden my understanding of the world of business. When I need a breather, I switch gears to explore psychology, astrophysics, and philosophy. Victor Frankl's "Man’s Search for Meaning" has deepened my understanding of the human psyche, Carl Sagan's "Pale Blue Dot'' has offered perspective on our place in the cosmos, and Epictetus's "Enchiridion" has shown me the power of one’s mind.

Occasionally, I take a long walk alongside the Seawall, watch Iron Man for the fiftieth time, and roast pork bellies for my friends.
