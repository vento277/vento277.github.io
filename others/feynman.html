Feynman Technique
2024 December
The Feynman Technique is a learning method popularized by physicist Richard Feynman. It involves four key steps:
  1. Select a concept and study it
  2. Teach the concept to someone else (or yourself) using simple, clear language
  3. Identify gaps in your understanding and refine your knowledge
  4. Repeat steps 1~3 until you get the hang of the topic
