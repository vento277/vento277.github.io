Does it work? Let's try it, and if it does work, fine, let's continue it. If it doesn't work, toss it out, try another one. - Lee Kwan Yew, Former Prime Minister of Singapore, in the 2007 The New York Times Interview


Buy

  1. Determine if the company is within the circle of competence.

  2. Develop a thesis and derive its intrinsic value using both quantitative measures and qualitative insights.

  3. Check the market price. If the market offers them below the calculated price, review the analysis to ensure nothing was missed.

  4. Compare the opportunity cost with the current portfolio holdings. If the potential gain is substantial compared to an investment, or holding cash, invest. If not, move on.
